Facilities Barpeta Law College, Barpeta

Library

The college Library is well equipped with more than 6,000 Books including Periodicals, Law Journals, Law Magazines, and Reference Books, etc. with the reading facility. Every Student can borrow two books for 15 days.


Sports

The college has sports facilities for all students.


Auditorium

The college has an auditorium facility for events and seminars.


IT Infrastructure

The college imparts legal Computer Education and for this purpose, there is a separate computer center with internet facility.

Moot Court

The college has the Moot Court facilities for all students.

Laboratories

The college has a departmental based laboratory facility for the students.
